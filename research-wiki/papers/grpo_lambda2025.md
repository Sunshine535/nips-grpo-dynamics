# Introduces token-level credit assignment to GRPO via eligibility traces (lambda-returns), improving reasoning task performance.

## Relevance to This Project
CSD interprets eligibility traces as temporal smoothing of the implicit teacher distribution (tau-plus smoothing), connecting credit assignment improvements to distillation signal quality.

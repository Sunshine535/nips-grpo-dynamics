# Proposes Self-Distillation Policy Optimization (SDPO) as an alternative to RL, using the model's own correct outputs as distillation targets.

## Relevance to This Project
They propose distillation as an alternative to RL. We prove RL IS distillation -- GRPO's gradient structure is inherently self-distillation, making SDPO a special case rather than a separate paradigm.

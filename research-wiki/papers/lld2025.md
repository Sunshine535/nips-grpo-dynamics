# Identifies the Lazy Likelihood-Displacement (LLD) death spiral as the mechanism behind GRPO training collapse in Search-R1.

## Relevance to This Project
CSD explains LLD as teacher quality degradation: when the online policy (implicit teacher) degrades, distillation targets become noisy, triggering a self-reinforcing collapse spiral.

# Introduces dynamic sampling and clip-higher mechanisms for stable large-scale GRPO training.

## Relevance to This Project
CSD explains DAPO's success: dynamic sampling filters out zero-signal CSD groups (all-correct or all-incorrect), and clip-higher prevents distillation overshoot -- both are implicit distillation quality controls.
